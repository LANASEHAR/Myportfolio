An editorial e-commerce experience designed for calm browsing—transforming online shopping into a serene journey.

4 WEEKS • FIGMA • RETAIL UX

The Vision

Paper & Soul is an immersive editorial platform for artisanal paper goods. In a market dominated by aggressive “Buy Now” tactics, the goal was to prioritize visual breathing room and storytelling to engage users who value the journey as much as the product.

Problem Statement

Most e-commerce platforms prioritize conversion over experience, resulting in cluttered interfaces filled with pop-ups, dense grids, and urgency-driven messaging. This creates cognitive overload and emotional fatigue, shortening browsing sessions and reducing meaningful engagement.

UX Objective

Design a calm, editorial shopping experience that encourages mindful browsing, emotional connection with products, and a frictionless path to checkout without pressure-driven tactics.

User Flow

  • Homepage discovery through curated editorial content
  • Product immersion with storytelling and material focus
  • Add to cart without disruptive prompts
  • Calm, step-by-step checkout
  • Post-purchase satisfaction and brand trust

The Editorial Interface

Homepage Editorial discovery over aggressive banners.
Product Immersion Storytelling-driven product detail.
Calm Checkout Clear steps, reduced anxiety.

Key Learnings

Paper & Soul demonstrated that reducing visual noise and designing for emotional comfort increases engagement and trust. Editorial layouts can transform e-commerce from a transaction into an experience.
