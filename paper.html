The Vision

Paper & Soul is an immersive editorial platform for artisanal paper goods. In a market dominated by aggressive "Buy Now" tactics, we prioritized visual breathing room and storytelling to engage creative professionals who value the journey as much as the product.

The "Clutter" Crisis

Traditional e-commerce overwhelms users with pop-ups and endless grids, causing 70% of users to abandon carts due to mental exhaustion. Browsing usually lasts only 2 minutes before frustration peaks.

70% Average Abandonment
2 min Frustration Peak

User Insights

Surveys of 25 creative professionals revealed a deep craving for "story-driven" discovery. Minimalism isn't just an aesthetic; it's a value multiplier.

  • Narrative Power: 85% of users engage longer with editorial layouts than standard grids.
  • Cognitive Load: Generous white space increases perceived product value by 40%.
  • Mindful Consumption: Users want to "feel" the texture of paper through high-quality visual hierarchy.

Persona: Lina

28, Reader & Creative Professional

Lina avoids cluttered sites that feel "salesy." She wants to browse like flipping through a beautiful magazine. She values aesthetics and meaningful discovery.

"I want to shop in a place that respects my attention, not fights for it."
